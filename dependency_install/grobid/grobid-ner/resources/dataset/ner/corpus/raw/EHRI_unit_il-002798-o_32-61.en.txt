Documentation of the murder of the Jews of Crimea, 1941-1942 Excerpts of documents of the Soviet Extraordinary State Commission (ChGK) in the Crimean occupied territory, including information regarding the murder of Jews in Yevpatoria, Zuya, Kheyrus and Biyuk-Onlar, names of those who perished, January-March 1942; notice from the mayor of Simferopol regarding housing for Jews, 15 March 1942; Ausweis (identity card) of Foma Paskitovski, from Kerch; copy of a photograph of a cell in the Kerch jail, December 1941.
